Hello

We are Carbono 06 and we want to give some details about the functionalities that our app offers you

First, you can create or schedule your daily meetings or events. You can also set your daily goals or your daily activities. The best part is that as the day goes by and you carry out or fulfill your goals and activities, you can check them. In this way you will never forget the important things of your day and any special date.

Select the activities with the highest priority

You can catalog the activities you have scheduled according to their priority

Delete the activities

Delete activities that you have already finished or have been discarded
